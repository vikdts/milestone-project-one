PADI Open Water Diver Beginner Course

This course includes PADI e-manual and a PADI certification card upon completion, equpment rental, 5 shallow water dives and 5 open water dives.


Requirements

The minimum age limit is 10 years, good physical health has to be ensured, as well as being a confident freestyle swimmer.


PADI

PADI open water diving license gives access to dive anywhere in the world, down to 18 meters depth.


PADI Open Water Diver Advanced Course

This course includes PADI e-manual and a PADI certification card upon completion, equpment rental and a torch for night dive, 5 adventure dives.


Requirements

The minimum age limit is 12 years, good physical health has to be ensured, as well as PADI certification card on completion of beginner course.


PADI

PADI open water diving license gives access to dive anywhere in the world, down to 30 meters depth with a buddy.


PADI Open Water Diver Divemaster Course

This course includes safety and risk management, assisting and supervising diving activities. Preparation of emergency assistance plan and PADI divemaster exam.


Requirements

The minimum age limit is 18 years, at least 60 logged dives, including diving experience in deep diving, night diving and underwater navigation. Completion of First Aid Care and CPR course within the past 24 months.


PADI

PADI open water diving license gives access to all necessery skills, in order to dive site, set up and management of young divers.
